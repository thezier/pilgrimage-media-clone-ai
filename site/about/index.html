About Me

Photographer + Videographer

My name is Mike Thezier (pronounced: tee-zee-ey) - my last name looks nothing like it sounds. I was born and raised in California and my life has taken me to places all over the world - especially France since half of my family live there. I really enjoy experiencing different cultures and places. My wife, Janna, is incredible and she just simply makes me better. We have three amazing children: Our oldest, Jameson, who is so intelligent and creative; Our middle child, Calvin, who is free-spirited and imaginative; And our beautiful and sweet baby girl Amelia - she’ll never do anything wrong.

I love the outdoors. If there’s any environment I prefer to be in, it would be anyplace with lots of trees, mountains, and hills. Janna and I have a love for traveling (both to new and familiar places) that we are excited to foster in our kids as they grow up. And I am a big coffee drinker (my wife is a coffee roaster, so I’m pretty spoiled).

It's my goal to make photographs that tell your story. No matter what shoot I do, I always want to create work that is genuine and compelling.

Thank you for visiting my website and if you'd like to know more about me and my work, please send me a message and let's start talking!
